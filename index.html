before you begin

This tool models drug concentration over time using numerical methods (RK4) applied to published pharmacokinetic parameters. A few important notes:

  • Parameters (absorption/elimination rates, volume of distribution, bioavailability, etc.) are drawn from existing pharmacokinetic literature, not from any individual measurement.
  • Some mathematical models are intentionally simplified. Due to the highly complex nature of these biological systems, full multi-compartment accuracy is partially out of the scope of this project, compounded by a lack of widespread literature to provide definitive equations or uniform constants.
  • Therapeutic, toxic, and lethal thresholds may be innacurate due to wide range of reported values.
  • Individual variation (metabolism speed, body composition, interactions, tolerance, and more) is not fully captured.
  • This tool is for educational exploration only and must not be used for clinical, medical, or safety decisions. Some values or assumptions may be incomplete or incorrect.
